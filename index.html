About Digital Manufacturing Program

Participants from five Fab Lab of Bangladesh, IUB, Dhaka, DUET, Gazipur, BAU, Mymensing, RUET, Rajshahi, and CUET, Chittagong are attending the Digital Manufacturing Program whch is arranged by AKGEC Skills Foundation at AKGEC-FABLAB. This is a short course of duration from 15th July, 2018 to 28th July, 2018. Overall contents of this program are campus tour to have primary induction about the scopes, 3D modeling in different software interface, html, 3D printing, Laser cutting, Embedded Programming, Wood Router, NI Lab View CORE, KUKA Industrial Robotics, and CNC machines etc.
